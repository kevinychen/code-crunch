There are N positive integers written on a chalkboard. Bob begins a tedious task:
he finds two numbers that are unequal, subtracts the larger number from the smaller number, replaces
the larger number with the result. Once all numbers are equal, he stops.

Turns out, Bob always stops. Moreover, he always with the same value. What is this magical value
on which the game ends?

Input Format:
N (number of numbers heh)
a1
a2
...
aN

Output Format:
Integer Answer


Example:

Input:
3
6 10 15

Output:
1
