Bob has been given a number, N. Bob is not satisfied with N and wants to try to make it bigger.
He can do so by rotating the number left or right, but he must rotate the number at least once.
Oh, and any leading zeroes at any stage must be removed.

What's the biggest number Bob can generate?

N <= 1,000,000,000

Input:
12345

Output:
51234

--

Input:
80

Output:
8

(Bob must do at least one rotation)
