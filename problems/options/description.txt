You're a TA for 18.314159, Intro to Arithmetic. For the final exam, you 
want to test your students on their ability do perform basic arithmetic.

The question format will be:

d1 _ d2 _ d3 _ d4 _ d5 = ?

Where d1-5 are digits and the blanks can be filled with one of +, -, *. The
students fill in the blanks and then do the arithmetic, writing down the answer they obtained.

TA's want to minimize grading work, so for given digits, find out how many unique answers
are possible. Standard order of operations applies.


Input:

1 _ 1 _ 0 _ 0 _ 0

Output:

3


Explanation: The possible results are 0, 1, and 2.
