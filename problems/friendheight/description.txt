You are sick and tired of switchin from looking up to looking down at your friends. Height diversity sucks and you've
determined to get rid of it. Given a pool of N candidates, you need to choose K of them
such that the biggest height difference is minimized.

K <= N <= 100
H <= 1,000,000

Input Format:
N K
H_1
H_2
...
H_N

Output Format:
Minimized Height Difference

Input:
5 3
7
2
4
6
9

Output:
3
