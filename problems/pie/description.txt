You like pie (or you do now). Your friends also like pie. You will eat an entire pie by yourself,
but your friends have other preferences, either "quarter", "half", or "big" (meaning three quarters).
Your friends are picky and want their piece of pie in a single slice or chunk.

What is the fewest number of pies you need to order for your friends AND yourself.

The first line of the input is N, where N is the number of friends.
Each of the subsequent N lines will have a size.

N <= 100

Input:
5
quarter
quarter
half
big
big

Output:
4
